Documentation of `std::xxx`
----------------------------
Depending on the compiler and C++-standard used, `nonstd::xxx` behaves less or more like `std::xxx`. To get an idea of the capabilities of `nonstd::xxx` with your configuration, look at the output of the [tests](test/xxx.t.cpp), issuing `xxx-main.t --pass @`. For `std::xxx`, see its [documentation at cppreference](http://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/container/xxx).
